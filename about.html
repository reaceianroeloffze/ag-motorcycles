About Us

Overview

Our company was established in September 2021, initially as Bike Spares Africa. In 2022 we re-branded to A & G Spares.

We offer a comprehensive and wide variety of spares and replacement parts for many delivery motorbike brands found on the streets in South Africa.

Service Quality

We pride ourselves on customer satisfaction through attention to detail and responsiveness to the needs of our customers.

If we dont have the stock you're looking for, we will order it from our wide network of suppliers and have it delivered wirhin 24 hours right to your physical address.

Affordable Prices

We sell affordable and excellently priced quality motorbike spares and replacement parts sourced from reliable and genuine suppliers who stock from original equipment manufacturers (O.E.Ms).

Service Guarantee

We offer a return back guarantee on all non-electrical spares bought from our shop that do not meet expectations.
